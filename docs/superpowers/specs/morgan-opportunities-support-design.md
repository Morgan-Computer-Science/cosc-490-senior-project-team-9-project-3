# Morgan Opportunities And Support Design

## Goal

Deepen the Morgan advising backend so it can answer student opportunity and support questions more specifically, not just degree-planning questions.

## Scope

This slice focuses on official Morgan support and opportunity paths for:

- career and internships
- scholarships and funding
- undergraduate research and experiential opportunities
- student success and academic support

## Product Outcomes

The app should get better at questions like:

- Where do I go for internships?
- Are there scholarships for Morgan students?
- How do I get involved in undergraduate research?
- What support exists for tutoring or student success?
- Who should I contact if I need help beyond my department?

## Data Strategy

Use official Morgan public pages and store:

- office/support paths
- opportunity programs
- named research/funding programs when publicly listed
- nearest valid contact path when the exact owner is not public

## Boundaries

This pass should not:

- invent scholarships or guarantees
- guess private or unpublished contacts
- replace degree-planning logic
- become a full university-wide scrape

## Success Criteria

- better grounded support and opportunity answers
- less generic fallback for internships, scholarships, research, and tutoring questions
- official contact or nearest valid Morgan path for each supported question type
